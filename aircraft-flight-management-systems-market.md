<p><strong>The Aircraft Flight Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CDU</li><li>MCDU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Flight Management Systems (FMS) market is primarily divided into two types: Control Display Units (CDU) and Multi-Function Control Display Units (MCDU). CDUs are basic units that assist pilots in navigation and flight planning through a simple interface, while MCDUs offer enhanced functionality, integrating multiple aircraft systems for seamless communication and control. MCDUs typically feature improved displays and user interfaces, supporting more complex operations, making them preferred in modern aviation. Both types are essential for efficient flight management.</p></p>
