<p><p>The Apple Cider Vinegar market is primarily divided into filtered and unfiltered types. Unfiltered apple cider vinegar retains the "mother," offering a cloudy appearance and stronger taste, appealing to health-conscious consumers for its probiotic benefits. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, driving market growth.</p></p>
