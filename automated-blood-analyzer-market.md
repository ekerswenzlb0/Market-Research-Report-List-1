<p><strong>The Automated Blood Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-part Differential Automated Blood Analyzer</li><li>Three-part Differential Automated Blood Analyzer</li><li>Five-part Differential Automated Blood Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Blood Analyzer market comprises three main types based on differential counting of blood cells. The two-part differential analyzer primarily focuses on distinguishing between two cell types, typically white blood cells and red blood cells. The three-part differential analyzer expands this to include lymphocytes, granulocytes, and monocytes, enhancing diagnostic capabilities. The five-part differential analyzer offers a more detailed breakdown, categorizing cells into five distinct types, providing comprehensive insights into blood composition and aiding in more accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
