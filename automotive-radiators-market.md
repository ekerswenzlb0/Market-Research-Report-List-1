<p><strong>The Automotive Radiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Down-Flow</li><li>Cross-Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive radiators market comprises two primary types: down-flow and cross-flow radiators. Down-flow radiators feature a vertical design where coolant moves from the top to the bottom, allowing effective heat dissipation due to gravity. In contrast, cross-flow radiators have a horizontal layout, with coolant flowing perpendicular to the air flow, enhancing cooling efficiency. Both types are vital for regulating engine temperature and ensuring optimal performance, catering to various vehicle designs and cooling requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
