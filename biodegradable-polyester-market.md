<p><p>The biodegradable polyester market encompasses various types, categorized into homopolymers and copolymers. Homopolymers include polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA), polyhydroxybutyrate (PHB), polylactic acid (PLA), and polycaprolactone (PCL), each exhibiting distinct properties for applications like packaging and biomedical uses. Copolymers, such as polybutylene succinate (PBS) and polyhydroxybutyrate-co-valerate (PHBV), offer enhanced performance through the combination of different polymer chains, providing versatility in product design. These materials cater to the growing demand for sustainable alternatives across various industries.</p></p>
