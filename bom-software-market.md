<p><strong>The BOM Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engineering BOM</li><li>Manufacturing BOM</li><li>Service BOM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BOM software categorizes into three main types: Engineering BOM (EBOM), Manufacturing BOM (MBOM), and Service BOM (SBOM). EBOM focuses on the product's design and development, detailing components, materials, and relationships in the engineering phase. MBOM outlines the necessary components and processes for actual production, ensuring efficient manufacturing workflows. SBOM targets the service and maintenance aspects, providing information on spare parts and repairs, ultimately enhancing product support and lifecycle management. These types streamline operations across the product lifecycle.</p></p>
