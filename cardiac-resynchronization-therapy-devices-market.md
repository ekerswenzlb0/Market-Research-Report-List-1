<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices are crucial for managing heart rhythm disorders. The market is primarily segmented into pacemakers, implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), and CRT devices. Pacemakers regulate heart rate, ensuring consistent cardiac function, while ICDs detect life-threatening arrhythmias and deliver shocks to restore normal rhythm. CRT devices specifically coordinate the heart's contractions, enhancing overall efficiency and improving heart failure symptoms. Each type fulfills distinct roles in treating varied cardiac conditions, significantly impacting patient outcomes.</p></p>
