<p><p>The Ceramic Metal Halide Ballast market is divided into two main types: double-ended and single-ended. Double-ended systems feature lamps with electrodes at both ends, offering higher efficiency and improved light quality, making them ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, single-ended ballasts have electrodes at one end only, providing a more compact design suitable for smaller installations. Both types serve various lighting needs in commercial and industrial settings, with their choice depending on application requirements, space constraints, and desired lighting effects.</p></p>
