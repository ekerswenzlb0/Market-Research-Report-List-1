<p><p>Ceramic milling cutters are specialized tools used in machining applications, categorized primarily into iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based types. Iron-based cutters offer high wear resistance and are cost-effective, making them suitable for general machining. Nickel-based cutters excel in high-temperature environments, providing excellent strength and oxidation resistance, ideal for aerospace applications. Cobalt-based cutters deliver superior toughness and are effective for difficult-to-machine materials, making them a preferred choice for intricate designs in advanced engineering sectors. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
