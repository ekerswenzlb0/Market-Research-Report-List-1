<p><p>The Color Selector market includes Chute and Belt Selector types, both essential in automated sorting processes. Chute Selectors use gravity to direct items through a series of chutes, sorting based on color as products fall. In contrast, Belt Selectors utilize conveyor belts to transport items past sensors that identify colors, facilitating controlled sorting. Both systems enhance efficiency in industries like food processing and recycling, ensuring accurate separation and improving overall productivity by enabling precise detection of color variations.</p></p>
