<p><p>Document storage and management software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software stores documents on remote servers, allowing users to access files from any internet-connected device, offering scalability and ease of collaboration. On-premises solutions, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and compliance but requiring more maintenance and upfront costs. Each type addresses specific organizational needs and preferences for data management.</p></p>
