<p><p>The heat insulation clothing market can be categorized into split type and Siamese type. Split type insulation clothing consists of separate components, such as jackets and pants, allowing for flexibility and layering according to varying temperatures. In contrast, Siamese type insulation clothing features a unified design, often resembling a jumpsuit, providing seamless warmth and reduced cold air intrusion. Each type targets different consumer preferences and usage scenarios, addressing specific needs in functionality, comfort, and protection against extreme temperatures.</p></p>
