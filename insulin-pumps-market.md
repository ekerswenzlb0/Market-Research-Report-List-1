<p><ul><li>Normal Pump</li><li>Patch Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin pumps market can be categorized into normal pumps and patch pumps. Normal pumps are traditional devices that deliver insulin through a tube connected to a catheter inserted under the skin. They offer continuous insulin delivery and can be programmed for personalized treatment. In contrast, patch pumps are compact, adhesive devices that attach directly to the skin, providing a more discreet option. These devices are designed for ease of use and enhanced mobility, catering to varying patient preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
