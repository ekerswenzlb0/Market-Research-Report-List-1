<p><p>Mountain dulcimers are categorized into two main market types: all solid wood and laminated wood. All solid wood dulcimers are crafted from a single type of wood, providing superior sound quality, resonance, and aesthetic appeal, often favored by professional musicians. In contrast, laminated wood dulcimers are made from layers of wood veneers, offering durability and affordability, making them accessible for beginners and casual players. Each type serves different preferences, budgets, and musical needs in the folk instrument market.</p></p>
