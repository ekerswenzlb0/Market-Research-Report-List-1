<p><strong>The Nerve Repair Biomaterials Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Nerve Repair/Neurorrhaphy</li><li>Nerve Grafting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nerve repair biomaterials market encompasses two primary applications: direct nerve repair (neurorrhaphy) and nerve grafting. Neurorrhaphy involves stitching severed nerves together using biomaterials that facilitate healing and restore function, crucial for injuries like cuts or traumatic nerve damage. Nerve grafting, on the other hand, involves using biomaterials to bridge gaps in nerves, often utilizing harvested nerve tissue or synthetic substitutes. Both applications aim to enhance nerve regeneration, reduce scar formation, and improve patient recovery outcomes.</p></p>
