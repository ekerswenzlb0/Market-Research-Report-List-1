<p><p>The organic soymeal market is primarily divided into two types: full-fat soymeal and defatted soymeal. Full-fat soymeal retains its oil content, offering higher energy levels, making it ideal for livestock feed and nutrition supplements. In contrast, defatted soymeal has had most of its oil extracted, resulting in a protein-rich product that is often used in food formulations, animal feed, and as a protein supplement. Both types cater to different nutritional needs and applications in the organic market.</p></p>
