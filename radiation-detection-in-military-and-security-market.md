<p><p>The radiation detection market in military and security encompasses various technologies designed to identify and measure ionizing radiation. Portable survey meters provide versatile field measurements, while personal radiation detectors (PRDs) serve for individual monitoring. Handheld dosimeters track exposure levels, and backpack-based radiation detection systems (BRDs) enable mobile assessment. Pocket-type instruments offer convenience for quick checks, while fixed, installed, automatic instruments provide continuous monitoring in specific locations. Vehicle-mounted radiation detectors facilitate assessment during transport, enhancing situational awareness and safety.</p></p>
