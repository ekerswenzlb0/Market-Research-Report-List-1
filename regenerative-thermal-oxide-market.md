<p><p>The Regenerative Thermal Oxidizer (RTO) market is primarily segmented into two types: rotary and compact systems. Rotary RTOs utilize rotating heat exchangers to efficiently capture and reuse heat, offering high thermal efficiency and reduced energy consumption, making them suitable for larger industrial applications. Compact RTOs, designed for smaller footprints, provide effective gas treatment solutions in limited spaces without compromising efficiency. Both types serve various industries aiming to reduce volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and enhance air quality while ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
