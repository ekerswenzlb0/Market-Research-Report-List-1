<p><p>Soft ferrite powders are crucial for various electronic applications, primarily categorized into Manganese Zinc (Mn-Zn) ferrite powder and Nickel Zinc (Ni-Zn) ferrite powder. Mn-Zn ferrite is widely used for transformers, inductors, and magnetic cores due to its high permeability and low losses at lower frequencies. In contrast, Ni-Zn ferrite is preferred for high-frequency applications like RF transformers and antennas, offering better performance at elevated frequencies. Both types play essential roles in enhancing the efficiency and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
