<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Non-Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical cryogenic pump market is primarily divided into submersible and non-submersible types. Submersible pumps operate submerged in the cryogenic liquid, providing efficient fluid transfer while minimizing vaporization losses. They are often used in storage tanks and other confined spaces. Non-submersible pumps, on the other hand, are installed above the liquid level, using seals and mechanisms to prevent leakage. They are suitable for applications where accessibility and maintenance are easier, making them favorable in various industrial settings.</p></p>
