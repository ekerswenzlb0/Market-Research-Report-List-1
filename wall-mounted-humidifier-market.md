<p><p>The wall-mounted humidifier market is primarily categorized into two types: vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers heat water to produce steam, releasing warm moisture into the air, which helps maintain comfortable humidity levels. On the other hand, water spray humidifiers disperse water droplets directly into the environment, providing a cool mist that enhances humidity without heating. Both types cater to different preferences and environmental needs, promoting improved air quality and comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
